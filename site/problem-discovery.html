Problem Discovery & Insights

Understanding the Problem

Before organisations can act effectively, they need to understand what is really happening.

I support organisations to explore challenges more deeply by combining research, behavioural insights, and collaborative sense-making.

What this involves

Types of Insight Work

This may include:

Qualitative Research

In-depth interviews, observations, and analysis to understand how people experience a problem.

Survey Design & Analysis

Designing and analysing surveys that generate meaningful, actionable data.

Behavioural Insights

Drawing on behavioural science to understand why people and organisations act the way they do.

Strategic Implications

Translating research findings into strategic implications that inform decision-making.

Insight-to-Action Workshops

Facilitated sessions that help teams engage with research findings and identify next steps.

The answer you are looking for is probably hiding inside the question you haven't asked yet.

Connecting research to action

Insight-Led Facilitation

One of my key areas of work is helping teams translate insights into meaningful decisions and action.

This often involves bringing research findings into facilitated workshops where teams can explore their implications and identify practical next steps.

The combination of rigorous research and skilled facilitation creates conditions for organisations to move from insight to informed action.
