Workshop Facilitation

I design and facilitate workshops that help teams explore complex challenges and work towards shared understanding and practical next steps.

My role as a facilitator is to create a structured and inclusive process that allows participants to engage with difficult questions, surface diverse perspectives, and move forward together.

Workshop Types

Types of Workshops

Workshops may focus on:

Strategy & Sense-Making

Helping organisations explore their strategic direction and make sense of complex situations.

Scenario Planning & Futures Thinking

Exploring possible future developments and their implications for decisions today.

Stakeholder Engagement

Designing and facilitating processes that bring diverse stakeholders into productive dialogue.

Organisational Alignment

Creating shared understanding and alignment across teams and leadership groups.

Collaborative Problem-Solving

Structured processes for exploring complex problems and developing practical responses.

Reflection & Learning

Creating space for teams and organisations to reflect on their work and learn together.

A room full of people thinking together is one of the strangest and most hopeful things in the world.

Online

Online Facilitation

I have extensive experience facilitating online workshops using digital collaboration tools such as Miro.

Well-designed online facilitation can enable deep engagement and meaningful collaboration across distributed teams.

Formats include:

  • Online strategy workshops
  • Remote futures workshops
  • Collaborative sense-making sessions
  • Hybrid events with both online and in-person participants

Depth

Multi-Day Processes

Some challenges require more than a single meeting.

I design multi-day collaborative processes that allow participants to explore complex issues in depth and develop new ideas through reflection, dialogue, and experimentation.

Examples include:

  • Strategy or innovation labs
  • Systems change workshops
  • Learning journeys
  • Cross-stakeholder dialogues
