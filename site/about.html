About

Facilitator and insights practitioner working with organisations navigating complex challenges.

My work focuses on creating spaces where people can explore problems together, surface meaningful insights, and develop practical ways forward.

Background

My professional background includes work across consulting, research, and collaborative strategy processes in both organisational and social impact contexts.
